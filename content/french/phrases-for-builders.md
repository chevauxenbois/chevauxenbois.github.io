A small list to expand over time.

- **Je construis**: I build.
- **J'apprends lentement**: I learn slowly.
- **Ce n'est pas fini**: It is not finished.
- **Il faut améliorer cela**: This needs improvement.
- **Une preuve**: A piece of evidence.

The phrase I like most for building in public is simple:

> Ce n'est pas fini.

It leaves room for iteration.
