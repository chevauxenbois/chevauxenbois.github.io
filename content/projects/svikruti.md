Svikruti scans repositories, websites, privacy notices, and consent journeys to create a review-ready privacy evidence pack.

It generates an HTML workbench, structured JSON, SARIF, RoPA starters, vendor registers, notice patch drafts, and fix-pack tickets.

## Links

- [Project site](https://chevauxenbois.github.io/svikruti/)
- [GitHub repository](https://github.com/chevauxenbois/svikruti)

## Why it exists

India needs practical privacy engineering tools that respect local regulation, local vendors, and the reality of product teams shipping quickly.
